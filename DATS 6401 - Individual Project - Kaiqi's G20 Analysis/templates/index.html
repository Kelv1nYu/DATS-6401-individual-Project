First Project of Data visualization.

G20 Analysis

In this project, we selected the population, gdp, education expenditure, military expenditure, and health care expenditure data of 11 G20 countries from 2012 to 2017, and performed relevant charts and analysis.

Relevant charts are divided into five categories: gdp, education, healthcare, military, and others based on relevant subject data.

The project uses html, css, javascript, jquery, Google visual interface to achieve.
